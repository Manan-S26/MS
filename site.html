My dear,

I hope you are thriving, every single time you read this — always. You should know that you are the most important person in my life and I love you so very much. I wish the World were perfect and you were reading this in front of me, but the World is just not ready yet I guess. I trust the Universe to correct it soon so you can be here with me and we can both make lots of money and achieve more than we have ever desired, all while coming back home to each other.

Today, I told 6 people, including my cab driver, that it's our anniversary, they didn't ask, I just felt like talking about you, as I usually do. It's funny that I talk about you when you are not there but like to stay quiet on our calls, I guess that's because I feel calm with you and love being in the moment, or maybe it's just because I like the slivers of silence amidst your yapping. All 6 people were happy for me and then all 6 people were sad for me, sad that we aren't together today. But I guess the World isn't ready yet. I trust the Universe to correct it soon so we can be together and achieve everything we never even dreamed of, while keeping people from being sad for us.

You make me the happiest I have ever been and I can't wait to spend all of my life with you. I do wish things were easier for us and we could just be together, that sounds easy enough… but I guess the World isn't ready yet. But I hear myself saying that and I realize the flaw. You are my World. The only one that matters, so I guess I just have to wait for the rest of the world to catch up to my World. To catch up to my love for you.

With love,
Bazz (short for Bablu Bindass)
